Kazuha

Kazuha is a wandering Samurai that travels with The Crux and often spoke in a poetic manner. Kazuha has a fairly carefree personality and enjoys reciting poetry. While not a picky eater, he doesn't care for hastily made food and prefers meals be made with care.

Hu Tao

Hu Tao is the 77th Director of the Wangsheng Funeral Parlor, a person vital to managing Liyue's funerary affairs, which she started at a very young age. She does her utmost to flawlessly carry out a person's last rites and preserve the world's balance of yin and yang. she has quite a funny mischivious personality. but many people hate her and she doesnt know why.
